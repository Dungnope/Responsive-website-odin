About me

Hi everyone! I'm Nami, the 'genius' navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates. Let's be honest: if it weren't for me navigating the Sunny through the crazy seas of the Grand Line, that idiot captain Luffy and the rest of my crew would have definitely been lost or sunk ages ago! The things I love most in this world are money (lots of Berries!), sweet tangerines from my hometown, and drawing accurate sea charts. My dream is to travel to every corner of the ocean and draw a complete map of the world with my own hands. Oh, and don't let these good looks fool you; if you dare lay a finger on my crewmates or my treasure, my Clima-Tact staff will give you a taste of thunder!
